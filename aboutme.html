About Me

Hello, my name is Naomi Iturbe Lopez. I am currently a freshman at Brigham Young University, the home of the color BLUE.
As part of my academic journey, I am a Pre-Business major who is pursuing admission into the Finance program. Though the world of business I wish to be a part of is often serious and highly professional, it doesn't stop me from having an unprofessional side.
As the daughter of a hard core University of Utah fan, I always had to hide my love for the color blue or I would get teased by my parents. However, now that I am at BYU, I can truly immerse myself in all things blue.
The color blue calls to many, if you are one of them, feel free to explore the links at the bottom of the page and get to know this fellow blue lover.





Here are some of my favorite things about the color blue:


  • It is one of the rarest colors in nature

    2. Less than 1% of the animal kingdon has blue pigment
    3. Most of animals with blue pigments are insects, marine life, birds, and reptiles/amphibians



  • Blue pigments go back in history

    1. Ancient Egypt created synthetic blue pigment from Lapiz Lazuli
    2. The French adopted the color blue as a symbol of power and prestige, replacing the color purple
    3. Prussian Blue was discovered by accident, becoming a cheaper, stable pigment used by Roman artists in 1709



  • Blue goes with sports

    2. It's one of the most popular team colors in college football
    3. My favorite college football team, the BYU Cougars, has blue as their team color
